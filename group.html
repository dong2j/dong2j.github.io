Join us

We welcome inquiries from prospective graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, undergraduates, high school students, and visitors. We are building an interdisciplinary group driven by curiosity about all things geological and planetary. Our group has expertise in both experimental and computational methods. Students and scholars from all backgrounds are welcome, including those from the geosciences, physics, chemistry, engineering, computer science, and beyond. Whether aligning lasers and diamonds in the lab or running quantum mechanical simulations at a café, we work together to explore research questions that build on your interests and invite you to grow across disciplinary boundaries. No prior experience in Earth or planetary sciences is required. If you're interested in exploring opportunities with us, please contact Junjie Dong at dong2j@caltech.edu.
