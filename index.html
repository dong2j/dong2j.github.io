My undergraduate honors thesis, constraining the wetting behavior of Fe-C melt in olivine, ringwoodite or bridgmanite and ferropericlase matrixes at Earth's mid-mantle conditions, finally got published and is now available online in Frontiers in Earth Science.

Our new paper (led by Dr. Feng Zhu), showing that metallic iron dehydrates hydrous wadsleyite at 15 GPa and 1400 ℃ and suggesting that global hydration and metal saturation are incompatible at the Earth's transition zone, is now available online in Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences.
